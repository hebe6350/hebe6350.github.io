The Fuel Your Future Networking Gala was a project for a client at Laurier. The client had a vision for their event to feature their brand colours - red and navy, and capture the sports theme without being too pushy. The execution of the designs for Fuel Your Future ended up with a very geometric futuristic look, playing on the popular themes in the sporting industry. This project also featured a facebook banner, an instagram sized graphic, and a large 1980x1080 graphic to be featured on the brands instagram story - a growing platform for the client.


The Sidelines project was a logo created for the same client at Laurier - SML. This graphic also played up the brands current colours while creating a fully new brand image that would be used as a second tier program for their organization. They were inspired by the graphics created for large organizations such as the CFL and MLB for specialty games - from the Grey Cup to the MLB All-Star game. This graphic was tailored to have a very similar feel.


The Lazaridis Students’ Society, another client of ours was in need of a design for their movember campaign t-shirts. They asked for a simple design that the team of volunteers could wear again, that featured their brand colours. This simple but effective design was a hit among their student audience.




The General Meeting campaign was created for Sports Management Laurier’s introduction meeting for general members. They wanted a design that tailored to the sports theme of their organization that as modern and eye-catching. The smoke effect of the background on the hockey arena tied together the sports theme and gave enough drama to be impactful.


The MLSE Case Competition was another campaign for the same client, featuring all the main details of the event for ease of the viewer. This geometric background was inspired by the modern theme of the sports industry and featured a player from the leafs - a main team of the event partner. This graphic was simple yet effective for our client, and the campaign featured multiple graphics from an instagram image to a cover photo to judge nametags



The O Day campaign was part of an initiative to help first year university students transition into University life at Laurier - hosted by our client. The campaign designs featured a background image of the school of business at Laurier and featured the brands primary colours. The centre O Day logo was also created specifically for this design, featuring a shadow on the O to give a 3 dimensional look. This project included multiple different pieces, including social graphics, designs for print and mail, email marketing, and name tag templates. This years O Day has a record number of attendees for our client.
